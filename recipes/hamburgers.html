Home Cooked Hamburgers

These tasty hamburgers will take you back to your childhood when you taste that delicious flavor that's only possible in a home kitchen.

Ingriedients

  • Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt

Steps

  1. First roll your beef into quarter pound balls, next patty out balls until a quarter inch thick.
  2. Salt pattys lightly, bring heavy bottom pan up to temp over medium heat.
  3. Now drop your pattys in and cook for 3 and a half minutes per side.
  4. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy.
